Hello World

David Dreggors david.dreggors@redhat.com 2021-05-27

The title, as cliché as it is in the software world, still seems fitting. After all this blog post is my first here and the internet is global, so it is my introduction to the world. Add to that the fact that most projects today start with a Hello World example. So here is mine.

I have spent the better part of my life dedicated to computers (hardware, software, and the use of). I have met some amazingly talented people along the way, and I still meet them. Along with camaraderie, friendship, and respect many have also given me knowledge and a healthy dose of patience along the way. It is in that spirit that I started this blog. I hope to return some of that sharing of knowledge, patience, camaraderie, friendship, and respect.

If any of this sounds good to you, GREAT! Welcome to my blog! If not, that’s ok too, I’m just glad you gave my blog a quick read to see if it held any value for you :-)

With that, I will say Welcome and HELLO WORLD!
